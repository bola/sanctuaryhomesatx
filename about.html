Our Story

Sanctuary Custom Homes was formed to deliver a calmer, clearer building experience. Our team brings decades of construction and project leadership to each home, pairing meticulous field execution with proactive communication.

We believe that building a custom home should be an exciting journey, not a stressful ordeal. That's why we've developed a structured process that combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology and enterprise-level project management.

From our roots in the Texas Hill Country to projects across Austin, we've delivered 60+ homes with a commitment to precision, transparency, and lasting client relationships. Our approach is simple: treat every project with the care and attention we'd want for our own homes.

Meet the Founders

Nick Chappell

Managing Partner

Project Delivery & Operations

With nearly 30 years in Texas construction, Nick has built a reputation for craftsmanship and reliability. From leading framing crews of 25+ to delivering multimillion-dollar estates and over 60 custom homes, his expertise spans every trade.

Nick has built strong pipelines with developers and investors while modernizing construction methods for greater efficiency. His ability to scale without losing attention to detail makes him a trusted partner for developers.

Experience: Nearly 30 years

Projects: 60+ custom homes delivered

Specialization: Field operations, quality control

Robert D. Corion

Managing Partner

Finance & Innovation

Robert brings more than 30 years of global executive experience, having led major energy, infrastructure and technology projects for some of the world's largest companies. Known for project management rigor, budgeting and vendor optimization, he is known for delivering complex developments on time and within budget.

Robert has managed $100M+ operations, introduced technology solutions to cut costs and speed delivery, and built strong vendor networks to ensure consistent outcomes—bringing enterprise-grade discipline to residential construction.

Experience: 30+ years global leadership

Expertise: Project management, budgeting, innovation

Background: Energy, infrastructure, technology

The Partnership

Nick and Robert combine extensive trade knowledge with executive project delivery expertise. Their approach blends craftsmanship with financial and operational discipline, offering developers a partner for both current and future projects.

This spirit of collaboration, at work and in their shared leisure time like fishing, reflects their belief that strong partnerships and long-term commitment are foundational for lasting homes and communities.
