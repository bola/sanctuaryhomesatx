Frequently Asked Questions

How Can We Help?

Find answers to common questions about building your custom home with Sanctuary.

Getting Started

How do I start the custom home building process with Sanctuary?

Begin by scheduling a consultation through our contact page. We'll discuss your vision, budget, timeline, and site details. If it's a good fit, we'll move into the preconstruction phase where we refine plans, finalize budgets, and secure permits.

Do I need to have architectural plans before contacting you?

No. While many clients come to us with existing plans from their architect, it's not required. We can recommend trusted architects and designers who align with your aesthetic and needs, and we work collaboratively throughout the design process.

What areas do you serve?

We build throughout Austin and the Texas Hill Country, including West Lake Hills, Westlake, Tarrytown, Zilker, Bouldin, Barton Creek, Dripping Springs, Wimberley, and Fredericksburg. If your project is outside these areas, contact us—we may still be able to help.

Process & Timeline

What's the typical timeline for a custom home build?

Most custom homes take 10-14 months from groundbreaking to completion. The preconstruction phase (design, permits, planning) typically adds another 2-3 months. Your specific timeline will depend on project complexity, site conditions, and finishes selected.

Can I visit the construction site during the build?

Absolutely! We encourage regular site visits. Your dedicated project manager will schedule weekly walkthroughs, and you're welcome to visit anytime with advance notice. We also provide photo updates and progress reports through our online portal.

How involved will I be in the building process?

As involved as you want to be. We provide regular updates and decision points throughout construction, but we handle all coordination, scheduling, and vendor management. Your main involvement will be selections, approvals, and weekly check-ins.

Budget & Pricing

How do you handle budget management?

We use transparent, line-item budgeting throughout your project. You'll have real-time access to costs via our project portal. Any change orders are documented and approved before work begins—no surprises at the end.

What's included in your estimates?

Our detailed estimates include all construction costs, permits, materials, labor, subcontractor fees, and project management. We'll clearly outline what's included and what's not (such as land costs, architectural fees, or specialty items) during the preconstruction phase.

Do you offer design-build services?

While we don't have in-house architects, we work closely with some of Austin's best design professionals and can recommend partners who align with your aesthetic and needs. We're experienced in both design-assist and plan-and-spec projects.

The Sanctuary Difference

What is Home Cloud™?

Home Cloud™ is our comprehensive digital handover system. At project completion, you receive a complete digital archive including architectural drawings, material specs, warranties, manuals, vendor contacts, and a complete photo timeline. It's accessible anywhere, anytime—perfect for future renovations or maintenance needs.

How does the 5-year concierge service work?

For five years after move-in, you have a dedicated concierge—your single point of contact for anything related to your home. We coordinate all warranty claims, schedule repairs, dispatch vendors, and provide maintenance guidance. It's like having a building expert on retainer.

What makes Sanctuary different from other custom builders?

Three things: our Home Cloud™ digital handover system, 5-year concierge service, and genuine commitment to long-term client relationships. 90% of our business comes from referrals—our clients become our advocates. Learn more on our Sanctuary Difference page.

Warranties & Support

What warranties do you provide?

We provide a comprehensive warranty covering workmanship and materials, plus manufacturer warranties on all products and systems. Our 5-year concierge service manages all warranty claims on your behalf—you make one call, and we handle the coordination.

What happens if I have an issue after move-in?

Contact your dedicated concierge directly (call, text, or email). We'll assess the issue, coordinate with the appropriate vendor or subcontractor, schedule the repair, and follow up to ensure it's resolved. You won't be left tracking down contacts or managing repairs yourself.

Do you handle remodels and additions?

Yes! We take on complex remodels and additions for both existing clients and new ones. Our structured process and attention to detail apply equally to renovation projects. Many of our remodel clients are referrals from satisfied custom home clients.
