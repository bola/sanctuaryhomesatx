Home Cloud™

Your Home's Digital Twin

At completion, you receive full access to Home Cloud™—a comprehensive digital archive of your entire project. Never search for a warranty document, paint color, or appliance manual again.

What's Included:
  • Complete Architectural Drawings: As-built plans and elevations
  • Material & Finish Selections: Every paint color, tile, fixture, and hardware spec
  • Appliance & Systems Documentation: Manuals, warranties, and maintenance schedules
  • Product Data Sheets: For every material used in your home
  • Vendor & Subcontractor Contacts: Direct lines to everyone who worked on your home
  • Photo Timeline: Complete photo documentation from site prep to final walkthrough
  • Warranty Information: Organized by system and expiration date

Access anywhere, anytime. Whether you're planning a renovation in 10 years or need to order touch-up paint today, Home Cloud™ has the answer.

5-Year Concierge Service

Your Lifetime Builder Advocate

From day one of ownership, you have a dedicated concierge—your single point of contact for anything related to your home. Not a call center. Not a ticket system. A real person who knows your project inside and out.

How It Works:
  • Direct Access: Call, text, or email your concierge directly
  • Warranty Coordination: We handle all warranty claims and scheduling on your behalf
  • Vendor Dispatch: Need something fixed? We orchestrate, you approve.
  • Maintenance Guidance: Seasonal reminders and proactive care recommendations
  • Future Projects: Planning an addition or upgrade? We're already familiar with your home.
  • Home Knowledge Transfer: Selling your home? We'll brief the new owners.

Included with every Sanctuary home. This isn't an upsell—it's our commitment to you and your investment.

Why This Matters

Building a custom home is one of the largest investments you'll ever make. Yet most builders treat it as a transaction—complete the project, collect payment, move on.

At Sanctuary, we see it differently. Your home will be part of your life for decades. Systems will need maintenance. Finishes will require touch-ups. Questions will arise. And when they do, you shouldn't be left searching for answers or tracking down long-gone subcontractors.

Home Cloud™ and our concierge service aren't just nice-to-haves—they're a fundamental shift in how we think about our responsibility to you. We're not just building your home. We're standing behind it for the long term.

That's the Sanctuary difference.
