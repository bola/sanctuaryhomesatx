Our Process

Building Methodology

A structured approach to delivering exceptional custom homes on time and within budget.

At Sanctuary Custom Homes, we've refined our methodology over decades of experience. Our approach combines traditional craftsmanship with modern project management tools, ensuring every project stays on schedule, within budget, and exceeds expectations.

Whether you're building a custom home from the ground up or undertaking a complex remodel, our structured process provides the roadmap for success.

Our Five-Phase Process

PHASE 1

Discovery & Preconstruction

Foundation for Success

Before breaking ground, we invest time understanding your vision, validating budgets, and establishing clear timelines.

What We Do:

  • Initial consultation and site analysis
  • Detailed cost estimation and budget validation
  • Review architectural plans for constructability
  • Establish project schedule with key milestones
  • Secure permits and approvals
  • Finalize contracts and project agreements

Your Experience: Clear communication from day one. You'll receive a comprehensive project plan, transparent budget breakdown, and a dedicated point of contact.

PHASE 2

Site Preparation & Foundation

Building the Base

Proper site preparation and a solid foundation are critical to every project's long-term success.

What We Do:

  • Site clearing, grading, and preparation
  • Utility connections and underground work
  • Foundation excavation and formwork
  • Concrete pour and curing with quality control
  • Foundation waterproofing and drainage
  • Initial inspections and approvals

Your Experience: Regular photo updates and site visits. You'll see the transformation from raw land to the foundation of your future home.

PHASE 3

Framing & Shell

Structure Takes Shape

This is when your home begins to take physical form. Our experienced framing crews bring precision to every wall, floor, and roof system.

What We Do:

  • Floor framing and subflooring
  • Wall framing and structural bracing
  • Roof framing and sheathing
  • Window and door installation
  • Exterior sheathing and weather barrier
  • Rough-in for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing

Your Experience: Watch your design come to life in three dimensions. Weekly progress meetings keep you informed as your home's skeleton takes shape.

PHASE 4

Systems & Finishes

Bringing It All Together

With the structure complete, we focus on the systems and finishes that make your house a home.

What We Do:

  • HVAC, electrical, and plumbing installation
  • Insulation and energy efficiency measures
  • Drywall installation and finishing
  • Interior and exterior paint
  • Flooring, tile, and stone installation
  • Cabinetry, countertops, and millwork
  • Fixture and hardware installation

Your Experience: This is where your selections come to life. Regular walkthroughs ensure every detail matches your vision.

PHASE 5

Final Details & Handover

Perfecting Every Detail

The final phase is about perfection. We meticulously complete every detail and prepare you for life in your new home.

What We Do:

  • Final inspections and code compliance verification
  • Comprehensive punch list completion
  • Detailed cleaning and final preparation
  • Systems testing and demonstration
  • Final walkthrough with you
  • Digital handover via Home Cloud™
  • 5-year concierge service activation

Your Experience: A seamless transition to homeownership. You'll receive complete documentation, warranty information, and ongoing support through our concierge service.
