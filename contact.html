Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical timeline for a custom home build?

Most custom homes take 10-14 months from groundbreaking to completion. The preconstruction phase (design, permits, planning) typically adds another 2-3 months. Your specific timeline will depend on project complexity, site conditions, and finishes selected.

Do you work with clients who already have architectural plans?

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with clients who have existing plans from their architect. During preconstruction, we'll review the plans for constructability and budget alignment before finalizing our contract.
